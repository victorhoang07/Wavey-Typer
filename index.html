Instructions

Wavey Typer is a typing-based games aimed to improve your typing speed and accuracy.

To begin, click Start. If you mess up and would like to restart, no worries, you can click Restart to do so.

There will be randomly generated words in the middle. Try to type as many as you can within the 30 second time frame.

As you play, enjoy the scene as it will change according to what you type!

In addition, there are sounds and music that accompany the game.

*HIGHLY SUGGEST SOUND AND MUSIC ON*
